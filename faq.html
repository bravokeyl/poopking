Why would I hire a poop scooper to scoop my dog's poo?

Because poo is gross! But really, dog waste can generate disease-causing bacteria that can make people sick. For instance, E Coli, a bacterium that can cause disease and fecal coliform bacteria. Dog feces will release excessive amounts of nitrogen into the soil as it breaks down, which results in brown patches or completely burn up parts of the lawn. But mainly, so that you and your family can enjoy a poop free yard!
